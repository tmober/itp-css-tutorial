About

On this website you should find information about how to write a psychological research report. Using this online tool, you should be able to create an outline for writing a research paper.

This online tutorial will walk you through some of the basic steps of creating a research report. Feel free to explore each section as you please and then return to make corrections and fix your design before submitting. Once you've submitted your ideas, you should be provided a .txt document which can serve as your outline for further report writing.
